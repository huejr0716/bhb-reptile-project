ABOUT US

We hope that your time here will be fun and that you find everything that you are looking for. Our goal is to provide our customers with the highest quality reptiles coupled with service that is second to none. We have been in business since 1989, but my love for animals stems back to my childhood.

Our slogan here at BHB is "Covering the Spectrum" and that's how our collection has been built. This approach has lead us to work with a wide variety of snakes from Pythons, including one of the most diverse Ball Python collections, to Boas as well as a wide variety of Colubrids and other reptiles. We try to maintain a collection that will offer animals for the beginning hobbyist all the way to the requirements of the serious investor. Working with such a diverse group of animals has also allowed us to be very competitive with our pricing of our animals.

We would like to thank all of our customers for their continued support, as well as our fellow breeders for their friendship and advice over the years. This business has been very good to us here at BHB and we look forward to what the future holds. Without you, our customers, none of this would have been possible and we will never lose sight of that.

We hope you enjoy your visit and please comeback often as our site will be updated frequently!

Brian & Lori Barczyk
